Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (or any cooking oil)
  • Optional: sesame seeds or green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, honey, and sesame oil.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Place samon fillets skin side down and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Flip the fillets, pour the sauce over, and cook another 3-4 minutes, spooning the sauce over as it thickens.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with sesame seeds or chopped green onions if desired.
